About BWC

The Belgian Waffles is one of the first waffle speciality stations in India that offers a unique, on-the-go waffle sandwich created fresh at our waffle stations with our specially formulated egg-less batter and premium fillings. BWC prides itself on continuous innovation while at the same time maintaining a consistent top quality product with a warm and dependable brand identity. Our waffles are freshly baked, hot and crispy and come in a variety of batters and fillings!

A burning passion for quality, consistency, novelty and adding value in the QSR space is the reason that our food franchise, The Belgian Waffle Co., came to being.
